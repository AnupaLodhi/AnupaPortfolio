Hi! I'm a pre-final year Computer Science undergraduate at SAGE University, Indore, working at the intersection of Explainable AI, software engineering, and digital marketing.

I developed an XAI framework integrating Isolation Forest with SHAP-based feature attribution for anomaly detection in banking audit workflows — published in IJEDR. I also independently built CareerPeak, a live AI-powered career guidance platform serving undergraduate students, and MediBridge, a healthcare communication platform connecting doctors, pharmacies, and patients through AI-powered prescription interpretation.

Currently, I am working on swarm intelligence-based multi-agent systems and exploring how Generation Z STEM students construct career identity in algorithmic environments.
